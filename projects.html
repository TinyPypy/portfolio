All You Need To Know About MokoSoko

Mokosoko is an all-inclusive, community-driven platform that assists artisans & fundis to turn their creativity into successful businesses. We are looking to use the artisan community's power to generate more interest & trust from potential buyers as a group than as single artisans trying to sell handmade items on their own. We aim to do this by providing artisans with the tools and resources necessary to help them sell more. We leverage the power of technology, i.e. an eCommerce platform that brings together artisans and lovers of handmade items. Artisans can register & list their items on our platform for free. We're doing this to help artisan communities who are largely underserved, to gain exposure to both local and international markets to empower them to sell more.
